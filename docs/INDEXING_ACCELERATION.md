# Indexing Acceleration

**Status:** Active  
**Scope:** Crawl/indexing acceleration without new route families  
**Last updated:** 2026-03-11

## 1) Sitemap Audit Summary

- Canonical families are present in sitemap generation, including:
  - state authority, state cost, city cost (rollout-gated)
  - appliance cost (state + rollout-gated city pilot)
  - bill estimator (hub/state/profile)
  - usage, fixed-kWh, comparison, and discovery hubs
- Rollout-gated families remain sourced from centralized rollout helpers.
- Discovery hubs are included with elevated weekly crawl hints.

## 2) Multi-Sitemap Segmentation Strategy

Sitemap generation is segmented deterministically in `src/app/sitemap.ts` using:

- `core`
- `states`
- `cities`
- `appliances`
- `estimators`

Rules:

- Segmentation is path-classification based and deterministic.
- URL assignment is mutually exclusive by segment classification.
- Duplicate URLs across segments are blocked by runtime assertion (`assertNoDuplicateSegmentUrls`).
- Sitemap index is published at `/sitemap-index.xml` and references generated metadata segments at `/sitemap/{segment}.xml`.
- The metadata sitemap family remains deterministic via `src/app/sitemap.ts` and `generateSitemaps()`.

## 3) Crawl Discovery Pathways

Internal linking and hub pathways prioritize canonical discovery:

- State-context link sections include bill estimator, appliance canonical routes, and discovery hubs.
- Energy comparison and electricity hub surfaces route users/crawlers into canonical clusters.
- Canonical clusters are linked back to discovery hubs to reduce crawl dead-ends.

## 4) Discovery Graph Strengthening

`public/discovery-graph.json` now explicitly includes pathway relationships for:

- state -> city relationships (`state_to_city_pathway`)
- city -> appliance relationships (`city_to_appliance_pathway`)
- estimator pathways (`estimator_pathway`)
- comparison pathways (`comparison_pathway`)
- hub-to-canonical-cluster discovery edges

This graph remains deterministic and generated by `scripts/knowledge-build.ts`.

## 5) Dataset Discoverability Signals

Dataset JSON-LD usage is standardized on major estimate templates:

- city electricity pages
- appliance cost pages
- bill estimator state/profile pages

These pages now include deterministic dataset distribution links:

- `/datasets/electricity-prices-by-state.json`
- `/datasets/electricity-prices-by-state.csv`

## 6) Verification Hardening

`scripts/verify-knowledge.js` includes indexing-acceleration checks for:

- sitemap segmentation signals and segment IDs
- canonical-family coverage signals inside segmentation logic
- discovery graph pathway integrity signals

These checks are lightweight and policy-focused; they do not change route ownership or rollout behavior.
